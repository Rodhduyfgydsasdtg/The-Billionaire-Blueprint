Using Excel, this project investigates the factors associated with billionaire success, uncovering relationships between wealth, industry dominance, self-made status, age, gender, and national economic indicators.

Storytelling Framework
Business Question

Can data explain why some people become billionaires while others do not?

Investigation

To answer this question, I explored:

* Which industries generate the most billionaire wealth?
* Are most billionaires self-made or inherited wealth?
* Which countries produce the highest number of billionaires?
* Is there a relationship between GDP and billionaire concentration?
* What age group dominates billionaire rankings?
* Does gender diversity exist among billionaires?

Key Findings

Finding 1

Technology and Finance dominate billionaire wealth creation.

These industries consistently generate the highest concentration of wealth among the world's richest individuals.

Finding 2

Most billionaires are over 50 years old.

Extreme wealth accumulation typically requires decades of business growth and investment.

Finding 3

A small number of countries produce the majority of billionaires.

Economic infrastructure, market size, and investment opportunities appear to play a significant role.

Finding 4

Self-made billionaires represent a substantial share of global wealth.

Entrepreneurship remains one of the strongest pathways to extreme wealth creation.
